About Us

Welcome to Youth Hope Circle

Youth Hope Circle (YHC) is a five-day leadership and English language training program designed to boost youth's social networks, leadership, friendship-building, goal-setting, decision-making, and problem-solving abilities. Along with team-building exercises and sporting events, the programs include leadership training, English immersion, seminars on pursuing educational opportunities, and media courses..

Youth ages (15-18) currently residing in Bangladesh are eligible to apply for the YHC program. The venue for this program is the Mati-ta Eco Resort in Chittagong

Develop youth leadership among participants from across Bangladesh

Strengthen participants independence and resiliency

Exposure to new and challenging experiences and activitie

Promote awareness, understanding, and action on gender equity
